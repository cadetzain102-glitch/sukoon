Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about connecting with Sukoon e Pakistan.

How do I join the community?
You can join by clicking the "Join Discord" button above or using the direct link: discord.gg/3WquAGR6r. Once you're in, introduce yourself in the #intro channel!
How do I open a ticket?
In the Discord server, navigate to the #complaints or #applications channel and click the "Create Ticket" button. Follow the prompts and provide the necessary information. A staff member will assist you shortly.
How can I apply for staff?
To apply for staff, open a ticket in the #applications channel. Requirements include: active daily, mature behavior, basic moderation knowledge, good communication skills, and loyalty to the community. Staff abuse is not tolerated.
What should I do if I have a complaint?
Open a complaint ticket in the #complaints channel. Please include: the username of the person involved, a clear description of what happened, and any screenshots as proof. The staff team will review and take appropriate action.
How do I become a partner?
To partner with Sukoon e Pakistan, open a ticket in the #partnership channel and mention "Partnership" in your ticket. We welcome collaborations that align with our values of peace, unity, and brotherhood.
Can I promote my server/content here?
Promotion is only allowed through the #partnership channel or via tickets. Unsolicited promotion in other channels may result in a warning or removal. Please respect our community guidelines and use the proper channels.
