Our Code of Conduct

We believe in peace, unity, respect, and brotherhood. These rules keep our community safe and welcoming for everyone.

1. Respect Everyone

Respect every member regardless of rank, background, or status. No personal attacks.

2. Keep It Peaceful

No drama, attention-seeking, or chaos. This community is built on sukoon and loyalty.

3. Privacy Matters

No leaking private chats, pictures, videos, or personal information of any member.

4. No Spamming

Spamming, flooding chats, or mass mentioning is prohibited. Keep chats clean.

5. Use Channels Correctly

Follow the purpose of each channel to maintain discipline within the server.

6. No Scamming

Scamming, fake deals, or misleading members results in an instant blacklist or ban.

7. No NSFW

No NSFW, gore, disturbing media, or inappropriate content. Keep it safe for all.

8. Respect Voice Chats

No mic spamming, loud noises, screaming, or ruining conversations in voice channels.

9. Respect Staff

Cooperate with moderation. Handle disagreements calmly through tickets, not public drama.

10. Loyalty & Respect

Loyalty and respect are valued more than fake popularity. Negativity is not tolerated.
